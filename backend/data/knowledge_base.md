# Children's Hospital Services

## General Services
Our hospital provides 24/7 pediatric emergency care, outpatient clinics, and specialized surgery rooms designed specifically for children.

## Vaccination Schedule
- **Hepatitis B**: Birth, 1-2 months, 6-18 months.
- **Rotavirus (RV)**: 2 months, 4 months, 6 months.
- **Diphtheria, Tetanus, & acellular Pertussis (DTaP)**: 2 months, 4 months, 6 months, 15-18 months, 4-6 years.
- **Influenza (Flu)**: Annually for all children aged 6 months and older.

## Financial Information
We accept most major insurance plans. Financial assistance is available for qualifying families.

## Visiting Hours
Parents are welcome 24 hours a day. Other visitors are welcome from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
